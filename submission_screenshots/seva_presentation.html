SEVA Platform06
Optimization Engine
ML predictions flow into operations research solvers.
Every deployment order is mathematically optimal, not heuristic. The MILP solver guarantees consistent, reproducible results every time.
Three Optimization Modules
MILP Allocation
OR-Tools solver allocates officers from 54 stations. Maximizes coverage, minimizes travel.
maximize: SUM(w[i] * x[i])
subject to:
capacity[s] >= assigned[s]
distance[s,j] <= max_km
SUM(x) >= min_coverage
  • Multi-station sourcing
  • Distance-weighted cost
  • Capacity constraints
Barricade Plan
Angular perimeter containment. Junctions scored by degree centrality + historical closure density.
BPR delay formula:
t = t_ff × (1 + 0.15×(V/C)⁴)
officer boost: +200 veh/hr
  • Degree centrality scoring
  • Angular containment
  • Queue length estimation
Diversion Engine
OSMnx-based corridor detours with delay estimation per alternate route. Real road network graph.
route_alt = shortest_path(
G, source, target,
weight='travel_time'
)
  • Corridor-specific routes
  • Travel time comparison
  • V/C ratio monitoring
